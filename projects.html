Sanitary Pads And Food Donation

The Rotaract Club of UMANG organized a sanitary pad donation drive to promote menstrual hygiene awareness and support underprivileged women. Through this initiative, we distributed essential sanitary products and educated beneficiaries on the importance of menstrual health. Our goal was to break taboos, encourage open discussions, and ensure access to hygiene products for those in need. With the support of our members and volunteers, we successfully reached numerous women, fostering a positive impact on their well-being. This initiative reflects our commitment to community service and empowering women through awareness and accessibility.

Breast & Cervical Cancer Awareness

The Rotaract Club of UMANG organized an online Breast & Cervical Cancer Awareness event to educate individuals, especially women, on risks, symptoms, prevention, and treatment. The goal was to spread awareness, empowering people with knowledge of early detection, lifestyle choices, and medical resources. Many cases go undiagnosed due to a lack of awareness, making such sessions vital. Hosted via Google Meet, the event featured Mrs. Mary Betwards, a leading oncologist, who covered causes, risk factors, screenings, vaccines, treatments, and advancements. The session included an interactive Q&A, ensuring participants could clarify doubts and engage actively.

The Mega Trek Fellowship

The Mega Trek Fellowship was organized on 8th June 2025 as a joint adventure fellowship by the Rotaract Clubs of the Nashik Region, including active participation from RAC Umang and RAC NYF. The purpose was to promote unity, healthy living, and stronger inter-club relations through an adventurous outdoor activity. Members gathered at ABB Signal at 6:00 AM and trekked together to the beautiful Anjaneri Fort, known for its mythological significance and natural beauty. The event was planned as a recreational break from routine, offering a platform for Rotaractors to bond, network, and embrace nature‚Äôs serenity. The trek fostered teamwork, conversations, and meaningful friendships while encouraging a fit and active lifestyle. Benefiting over 40+ Rotaractors, the project successfully strengthened regional Rotaract ties and left members with unforgettable memories and renewed enthusiasm for upcoming collaborative projects.

Secret Santa Gift Exchange 2024

The Secret Santa Gift Exchange is organized by RAC Umang Nashik to spread joy and strengthen camaraderie among members. Participants will register by December 15, 2024, and receive the name of their gift recipient anonymously. The gift unveiling and fellowship event will be held on December 26, 2024, where members will exchange gifts and enjoy an evening filled with fun and bonding activities. The purpose of this project is to foster a sense of togetherness, boost morale, and provide a platform for social interaction during the festive season. The project aims to benefit approximately 30-50 Rotaract members from different clubs within the RI 3030 District, enhancing their club experience and personal connections.

Fit For Impact 1.0

The program consists of a series of training sessions, each focusing on a different aspect of fitness, including warm-up exercises, yoga stretching, aerobics, and cool-down stretches. This project will benefit approximately 50 community members, including individuals of all ages and fitness levels. By participating in this program, community members can expect to improve their overall fitness, reduce stress and anxiety, and develop healthy habits that will last a lifetime. The project is a collaborative effort between the Umang Rotary Club and the MSG Copper Club, and is expected to have a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of community members.
