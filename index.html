About Us

Rotaract Club Umang is a vibrant community of young leaders dedicated to service, leadership, and fellowship. We strive to make a positive impact through community projects, professional development, and global connections. As we step into another inspiring year, we are thrilled to welcome you to the latest edition of RAC UMANG’s magazine! The 2025/26 theme, “...” perfectly encapsulates the spirit of our journey – a journey of unity, compassion, and transformation.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower youth to serve communities and foster sustainable change while connecting with others who fight for the same.

Our Vision

A world where young leaders create lasting impact through service and collaboration. A world where people aren't burdened to help one another but do it to better improve the lives of others.
